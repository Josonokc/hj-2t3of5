Running

Anchored to your paces: easy 7:00/km, threshold ~5:45–6:00/km.

Thursday — quality (alternate weekly)

  • Tempo: 2 km easy WU → 4–5 km @ ~6:20–6:35/km → 1 km easy CD
  • Intervals: WU → 6–8 × 600–800 m @ ~5:45–6:00/km, jog 90 s recoveries → CD

Sunday — long run (key driver)

  • Start 12 km @ easy 7:00/km (Z2, conversational).
  • Add ~1 km/week; every 4th week cut back ~30% to recover.
  • End of Phase 1 ≈ 18–20 km. Keep easy runs truly easy.

Progression — double progression

  • Lifts: hit the top of the rep range on all sets at the prescribed RIR → add load, reset to the bottom. Hold squats/hinges at RIR 2–3 to protect the back.
  • Runs: long run +~1 km/wk (with the 4th-week cutback); nudge tempo pace / interval count only when the prior week felt controlled.
  • Deload ~week 6 (or sooner if the back flares): cut lift volume ~40%, shorten the long run.

Back-care & hip mobility

Addresses the suspected tight hip flexors — do daily-ish, and before Lower & long-run days.

  • Couch stretch / kneeling hip-flexor stretch — 2 × 30–45 s/side
  • 90/90 hip rotations, glute bridges, dead bugs
If the lower back flares: drop spinal loading (hack squat + hip thrust / machine hinges), keep training, and flag it at /check-in.

  • Phase 2 adds compromised running (run → station → run) and full-station simulations.
