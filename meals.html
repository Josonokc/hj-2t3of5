How to use it

  • Pick one option per slot (breakfast, lunch, dinner, snack) + add-ons to top up.
  • Protein naturally runs high with your foods (chicken / eggs / yogurt / whey) — that's fine. Carbs are the lever you move around training.
  • Hit protein every meal; put the biggest carb servings around Thu quality + Sun long runs.

Run-day fueling (key for the 30 km / marathon build)

  • Runs < 60 min: no fuel needed — just hydrate.
  • Runs > 75–90 min: practice ~30 g carb / 45 min (1 gel, or banana + sports drink). Start early, not when empty — train the gut now.
  • After hard/long sessions: protein + carbs within ~1–2 h (whey + fruit, or your next meal).
  • SG heat: add electrolytes on long runs (sodium especially); occasionally weigh before/after to gauge sweat loss.
